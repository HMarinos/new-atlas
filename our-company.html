ABOUT US

Client-focused, partnership-based approach

Atlas Property Partners was founded with the purpose of offering multi-disciplinary real estate expertise that is closely aligned with our clients’ interests. As Atlas is not a brokerage-led business, our advice is impartial, transparent and fully focused on the needs of investors and owners to optimize the value of their real estate holdings.

Offering extensive pan-European experience in fund management, private equity and asset management, we partner with our clients to help them navigate the Greek real estate market with intellectual rigour, transparency and accountability.

Our Vision

To be the leading real estate investment partner in Greece to institutional funds and property owners.

Our Mission

To deliver best in class services to our clients, and create trusted, collaborative partnerships with our stakeholders.

Our Values

Excellence Innovation Integrty Huminity

MEET OUR TEAM

The Atlas team comprises skilled professionals who are subject matter experts in their respective fields. Together, every member of our team reflects and upholds the values of the company which is centered on integrity, excellence and humility.

Panagiotis Chionas
Senior Associate

Panagiotis brings significant underwriting, advisory and financial modelling expertise to Atlas. Prior to joining Atlas, he led large underwriting projects of NPL portfolios in Greece and Cyprus. Panagiotis started his career in IT software and business development. Having operated in the MENA region, he brings an international perspective and best practices from other industries to our business. Panagiotis holds a Bachelor’s Degree in International Relations from the University of the Aegean and is a CFA® charterholder.

George Tingis
Managing Director

George is the founder of Atlas. He offers significant thought leadership and subject matter expertise with decades of European experience in real estate investment, asset management and credit risk analysis. Prior to founding Atlas, George was Director and Head of Greece for Värde Partners, a global alternative investment manager. He has also held senior roles in London with Värde Partners and Lehman Brothers (Real Estate Private Equity), asset managing and successfully exiting equity and debt positions in excess of EUR4 billion across Europe. As Principal of Castor REA, George led specialist advisory projects on behalf of Blackrock and PIMCO to assess the real estate loan positions of the systemic banks in Greece and Cyprus. George holds an MBA (with Distinction) from London Business School, MSc in Economics & Finance from the University of York and is MRICS-qualified.

Vasileios Gkionis
Head of Asset Management

As Head of Asset Management, Vasileios has overall responsibility for the performance of all assets under management, including development projects. Vasileios is a civil engineer with over 15 years of experience in real estate development, valuation, and asset management. He has worked with the largest financial institutions in Greece and founded a development company specializing in residential projects. Vasileios holds a Bachelor of Science in Civil Engineering from Democritus University of Thrace. He is a member of several professional organizations including the Technical Chamber of Greece (TEE), the Hellenic Society of Civil Engineers, the Hellenic Society of Appraisers (SEKE), the Hellenic Institute of Evaluation (ELIE) and the Institute of Zero Energy Buildings (INZEB). Vasileios is also a Certified Real Estate Appraiser Recognized European Valuer – TEGoVA and Certified DGNB Auditor by the German Sustainable Building Council.
