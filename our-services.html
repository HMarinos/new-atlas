OUR SERVICES

Atlas Property Partners are subject matter experts in direct and indirect real estate assets across all sectors. We partner with investors and clients across the real estate capital spectrum and offer them ways to access the Greek real estate market.

Fund Management

Buy-side support to investors throughout the asset lifecycle

Atlas acts as the local operating partner for institutional investors through discreet mandates. We align our interests with those of our clients in all steps of the investment life cycle. Typical services include:

  • Advise on optimal holding company structure
  • Source opportunities suitable to clients’ risk-return profiles
  • Manage the closing of investments
  • Source and negotiate debt facilities
  • Intensive management of assets
  • Management of the company structures
  • Disposal of the assets and distribution of cash to investors

Asset Management

Creating and unlocking value, asset by asset

Atlas acts as the strategic asset manager for institutional investors and financial institutions to unlock and deliver value on their real estate collateral. Typical services include:

  • Management of REOCOs
  • Legal and technical due diligence
  • On-boarding of assets
  • Creation of asset business plans
  • Lease up of vacant areas
  • Property management and insurance
  • Strategic capex
  • Asset repositioning
  • Preparation and monetisation of assets

Advisory

Underwriting and analysis of potential NPL and REO transactions

Atlas advises third parties on value-enhancing strategies for either existing assets or targeted acquisitions. We draw from a depth of market knowledge and network of partners to provide objective and impartial advice on a range of services:

  • Portfolio segmentation and recovery analysis
  • NPL and REO valuations
  • Highest and Best Use analysis
  • Investment appraisals
  • Due diligence of single-asset, portfolio or non-performing loan transactions
  • Strategic and business advisory
  • E-Auction credit bid analysis
