Welcome to Atlas
Property Partners

A trusted partner in Greece

Atlas Property Partners is an independent real estate investment management and advisory firm based in Athens, Greece.

We combine a multi-disciplinary team with a client-focused, partnership-based approach to help our clients realize their real estate capital objectives.

With extensive pan-European experience in real estate private equity, fund and asset management, Atlas offers our clients a broad spectrum of skills combined with a disciplined and analytical approach to real estate investment management.

Atlas operates across all real estate sectors and asset types, including direct property, non-performing loans (NPL) and real estate owned assets (REOs).

''Our mission is to deliver best-in-class services to our clients, and to create trusted and collaborative pertnerships with all our stakeholders.''

Our Services

Identify. Create. Realize. Value.

Atlas operates three business lines through which clients can identify, create and extract optimal value and performance from their real estate portfolios.

Fund Management

Through discreet investment management mandates, Atlas acts as the local operating partner for institutional investors.

From acquisitions to financing, developments and hands-on asset management all the way through to the monetisation of assets, Atlas are partners with our clients at every stage of the investment life cycle.

Advisory

Through underwriting, due diligence, analytics and in-depth market knowledge, we advise on optimal pricing, deal and financing structures for potential REO or NPL transactions.

Asset Management

Atlas acts as the strategic asset manager for institutional investors and financial institutions looking to unlock and deliver value on their real estate collateral and REOs.
